01

MONDAY — ACTIVATION DAY

Wake the body. Learn the ball. Set the rhythm.

🌅 Morning · 50–55 min

FULL BODY ACTIVATION + STRETCH

Dynamic warm-up → Bodyweight conditioning → Mobility cool-down

Dynamic Warm-Up — 8 min

1
High Knees 30 sec × 3
Drive knees above hip height. Arms pump. This activates hip flexors and elevates heart rate.
2
Leg Swings (front/back + lateral) 15 reps each leg
Hold wall for balance. Swing controlled through full range. Opens hip joint capsule.
3
World's Greatest Stretch 5 reps each side
Lunge forward, drop elbow to floor, rotate upper body skyward. Hip flexor + thoracic all-in-one.
4
Ankle Circles + Calf Raises 15 reps each direction
Prepares ankles for lateral movements. Critical for dribbling and cutting.

Bodyweight Conditioning — 30 min

5
Bodyweight Squats 4×15 reps · 45s rest
Feet shoulder-width, toes slightly out. Chest up, drive through heels. Builds quad and glute power for explosive jumping and sprinting.
6
Push-Ups (standard → wide → diamond cycle) 3×12 each variation
Upper body strength for shielding the ball, throw-ins, and collisions. Keep core tight — no sagging hips.
7
Reverse Lunges 🦶 4×12 reps each leg
Start with LEFT leg stepping back. Single-leg stability and quad/glute strength. Critical for striker planting and shooting.
8
Plank Hold 3×45 seconds
Tight core = controlled dribbling and better balance at speed. Don't let hips drop or rise.
9
Glute Bridges 3×20 reps · slow 2s up, 2s down
Lay on back, feet flat, drive hips up squeezing glutes. Prevents hamstring injury and powers your sprint.
10
Lateral Hops (side to side) 3×20 hops each direction
Small quick hops off one foot. Builds ankle stability and lateral movement — essential for Doku-style cuts.

Mobility Cool-Down & Stretch — 12 min

Standing Hamstring Stretch 45 sec each leg
Straight leg on surface at hip height, lean forward gently. Critical for preventing hamstring pulls during sprinting.
Hip Flexor Lunge Stretch 45 sec each side
Half-kneeling position, lean hips forward gently. Unlocks stride length and reduces lower back tension.
Seated Groin / Adductor Stretch 60 sec
Sit on floor, soles together, gently press knees toward floor. Essential for all-direction dribbling.
Figure-4 Piriformis Stretch (Knee health) 45 sec each side
Lay on back, cross ankle over opposite knee, pull both toward chest. Releases piriformis — reduces knee strain by aligning hip rotation.
Calf Doorway Stretch 30 sec each calf
Hands on wall, one leg extended back, heel flat. Prevents Achilles and ankle injury.
Child's Pose + Deep Breathing 60 sec
Kneel, sit back on heels, stretch arms forward on ground. Decompress spine. Deep belly breaths reset nervous system.
⚽ Afternoon · 55–60 min

BALL MASTERY + WEAK FOOT INTRO

5×10 ft space. Just a ball. This is where technical genius begins.

Ball Warm-Up — 5 min

🦶 Sole Rolls — Left Foot Only 2 min
Roll ball side to side under your left sole only. Stay on toes. This is your LEFT FOOT awakening drill. Do this every single day until it feels natural.
Toe Taps (both feet alternating) 2 min continuous
Tap top of ball alternately with each foot. Light, quick, rhythmic. Stay close to ball. Develops touch and coordination.

Weak Foot Block — 20 min 🦶

🦶 Left Foot Juggling (seated then standing) 5 min
Sit on chair, drop ball, juggle only with left foot. Don't worry about count — focus on consistent clean contact. This trains neural pathways. Progress: 5 juggles → 10 → 20. When you hit 10 consistently, stand and do it standing.
🦶 Left Foot Wall Passing (inside of foot) 8 min
Stand 1.5 meters from wall. Pass with inside of LEFT foot only. Let ball return, pass again. Focus on the angle of your ankle — lock it. Aim for the same spot on the wall every time. This is exactly what Olise drilled to make his weak foot lethal.
🦶 Left Foot Ground Shooting into wall 7 min · 50 shots
Stand 3m from wall. Strike ball firmly with LEFT foot into wall. Plant foot beside ball, follow through. Don't power it — precision first. Start slow, build pace over the 7 minutes. Count your strikes. Goal: 50 clean contacts.

Ball Mastery Drills — 20 min

Inside-Outside Touch (both feet) 5 min
Tap ball with inside right → outside right → inside left → outside left. Keep ball close. This is Neymar's bread and butter — mastery of all four surfaces of the foot.
🦶 V-Pull (left foot dominant) 5 min
Step on ball with left sole, pull back diagonally, push forward with same foot. V-shaped motion. Simulates Neymar's directional change move. Do 70% left foot, 30% right.
Cruyff Turn Practice 🦶 5 min
Fake to pass/cross, drag ball behind standing leg, turn 180°. Drill LEFT foot as the dragging foot. Yamal uses this under pressure. 5 turns left, 2 turns right — always bias left.
Step-Over Right → Cut Left 🦶 5 min
Step over ball with right foot, then accelerate left with LEFT foot touch. This is the Doku pattern — step over to create space, burst left with weak foot. Repeat 10 reps, rest 20 sec.

First Touch Training — 10 min

🦶 Left Foot Cushion Trap vs Wall 5 min
Throw ball against wall (or kick gently), receive it with sole of left foot. Cushion it dead. Don't let it bounce away. "Soft foot" is the goal — feel the ball, don't meet it hard. This is how Yamal receives under pressure.
Aerial Chest → Left Foot Control 5 min
Throw ball up, chest it down, control with LEFT foot. Repeat. Elite first touch = the ball going exactly where you want it. Focus on your body shape when receiving.
02

TUESDAY — LOWER BODY POWER + DRIBBLING

Build legs like Doku. Dribble like Neymar.

🌅 Morning · 55 min

LOWER BODY EXPLOSION + HIP MOBILITY

Warm-Up — 8 min

1
Jumping Jacks 2 min continuous
Classic full-body primer. Elevates heart rate, warms shoulders and hips.
2
Hip Circles (standing, large circles) 20 each direction
Feet shoulder-width, hands on hips. Big slow circles. Lubricates hip socket before heavy lower work.
3
Inchworms 8 reps
Walk hands out to plank, walk feet back up. Full posterior chain stretch + shoulder activation.

Lower Body Circuit — 32 min (3 rounds)

A
Jump Squats 3×12 reps · 60s rest
Squat down, explode upward as high as possible, land softly. Builds explosive power for jumping headers and acceleration. This is how you jump over defenders.
B
Bulgarian Split Squats 🦶 3×10 each leg · start LEFT
Rear foot on chair/step, front foot forward. Lower until front thigh is parallel. Incredible single-leg strength for cutting, pivoting, and finishing.
C
Calf Raises (single leg) 🦶 3×20 each leg · start LEFT
Stand on edge of step, rise on toes, lower below step. Builds calf power critical for sprinting and ankle stability during dribbling.
D
Lateral Shuffle Bursts 3×30 sec
Side shuffle rapidly 5 steps left, 5 steps right. Low athletic stance. This is pure Doku agility training — change direction instantly.
E
Single-Leg Glute Bridge 🦶 3×15 each leg · start LEFT
One foot flat, other leg raised. Drive hips up. Targets glute activation imbalance between legs. Essential for equalizing your weak-foot power.

Hip & Hamstring Stretch Protocol — 15 min

Lying Hamstring Stretch (towel assist) 60 sec each leg
Lay on back, loop towel around foot, pull leg toward ceiling. Knee STRAIGHT. Deep hamstring stretch. Hold — don't bounce. This is the most important injury prevention stretch for footballers.
90/90 Hip Stretch 90 sec each side
Sit on floor, both legs at 90° (front knee and back knee). Lean gently over front leg. Opens external and internal hip rotation — fundamental for full dribbling agility.
Pigeon Pose (modified) 60 sec each side
From plank, bring one shin forward between hands. Lower hips toward floor. Elite hip opener. Protects knees by releasing tension from the IT band and glute complex.
Standing Quad Stretch 🦶 45 sec each leg · hold LEFT longer
Balance on one foot, pull opposite ankle to glute. Hold LEFT leg 5 seconds longer than right — active weak-side balance training.
Knee Circles + VMO Activation 20 circles each direction
Stand, slight knee bend, hand on knee, make slow circles. Then terminal knee extensions (extend knee from 30° to full lock). Activates the VMO muscle — the key to knee stability in football.
⚽ Afternoon · 55 min

DRIBBLING MASTERY + WEAK FOOT WALLS

Ball Warm-Up — 5 min

🦶 Left Sole Rolling — Eyes Closed 2 min
Same as Day 1 but now close your eyes. This activates proprioceptive touch — feel the ball without looking at it. Elite players never look at the ball.
Toe Taps Speedwork 3×30 sec max speed
Go as fast as you can. This is pure coordination speed. Count your taps. Beat yesterday's number.

Weak Foot Block — 20 min 🦶

🦶 Left Foot Laces Shot vs Wall 10 min · 60 shots
Now use the LACES (top of the foot). Strike clean through the middle of the ball. 3m from wall. Power shot with left foot. Lock ankle, lean slightly forward. This is what Olise's weak-foot goals look like. Start slow, increase power each set.
🦶 Left Foot Outside Touch Dribble 5 min
In your 5×10 space, dribble in a figure-8 pattern using ONLY the outside of your left foot. This is the Neymar touch — outside of the weak foot. Feel awkward? Good. That's your brain building new pathways.
🦶 Left Foot Cone Slaloms (use objects) 5 min
Set up 4-5 objects (shoes, bottles) in a line within your space. Dribble through using only left foot touches. Touch every other object on the left side, alternate on right side. Slow and controlled first, then build speed.

Dribbling Technique — 20 min

Scissors Stepover (both feet) 8 min
Classic scissors — step right foot over ball to the left, then go right, or vice versa. Practice slowly first. Then double scissors (two in a row). This is Neymar's most common 1v1 move. Do 60% to the RIGHT (cuts left onto your weak foot).
Elastico Intro 🦶 7 min
The most Neymar skill of all. Ball control with outside of foot → snap back with inside of same foot to change direction. Start with right foot to learn the motion. Then LEFT foot. Focus on the snap — it must be quick. This takes days to click, so just rep it.
Speed Dribble in tight space 5 min
In your 5×10 ft, dribble at maximum speed back and forth, alternating feet each touch. Ball must stay within 30cm of your feet. Simulates Doku's close control at pace. Keep head up for last 2 minutes.

Creative Finishing — 10 min

🦶 Left Foot Curling Practice vs Wall 10 min · 40 shots
Inside of left foot, strike the right half of the ball. Follow through across your body. Feel the ball spin. This is how Olise curls it into the top corner. 3m from wall. Aim for a specific mark. Build muscle memory.
